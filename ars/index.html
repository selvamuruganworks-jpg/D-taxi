Welcome to Sri Arvind

Nurturing Leaders of Tomorrow

Founded with a vision to create a stimulating home-away-from-home, Sri Arvind Nursery & Primary School is a leading educational institution focused on child-centric development. We encourage children to observe, question, experiment, and excel in every aspect of their growth.

Through individual care, high teacher-to-student ratios, and dedicated character education, we ensure our young learners start their academic journey with high confidence.
