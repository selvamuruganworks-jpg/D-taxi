Admission Guidelines

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds who demonstrate a readiness to engage with our play-focused and primary curriculum.

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: 2.5 to 3.5 years by June 1st
  • LKG: 3.5 to 4.5 years by June 1st
  • UKG: 4.5 to 5.5 years by June 1st
  • Class 1: 5.5+ years by June 1st

Required Documents

  1. Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  2. Passport size photographs (Child - 4, Parents - 2)
  3. Immunization & Vaccination Records
  4. Proof of Address (Aadhaar, Utility Bill)
  5. Previous school's transfer certificate (Class 1-5)
