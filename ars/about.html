About Sri Arvind School

Excellence in early education and values since 2012

Our Vision

To be a leading early-education institution that fosters intellectual curiosities, moral foundations, and values in every child, paving their path to become global citizens and future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and high-quality learning ecosystem where preschool and primary kids develop critical thinking, physical abilities, social qualities, and an enduring love for knowledge.

Leadership Insights

A closer look at the values guiding our institution

Rev. Sister Beatrice
School Correspondent

Welcome to Sri Arvind School. We believe that childhood is a sacred phase of exploration and character growth. Our mission is to combine standard academic values with early childhood play methodologies to allow kids to flourish. We focus on shaping character alongside intellect, preparing them not just for examinations, but for life itself.

Our teachers are fully dedicated to child counseling and physical growth metrics. We pray that every child entering Sri Arvind's finds the inspiration to seek knowledge, values, and noble goals.

Ms. Sarah Jenkins
School Principal

At Sri Arvind School, we recognize that every kid has a unique pace and cognitive style. Our classrooms are modeled to promote active learning. We integrate block-building, picture guides, read-aloud circles, and computer interfaces to capture interest and develop calculation foundations.

We work in close cooperation with parents. Our Portal system provides daily progress reports, direct messaging, and attendance logs. Let's work together to provide our kids a memorable, productive primary school experience.
